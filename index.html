About me

I'm Peter Hebden (Pit), a masters student of Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Birmingham, UK. I'm a programmer by background, having been writing code recreationally my whole life. My primary interests these days are in semantics of programming languages and functional programming.

You've found piturnah.xyz, which is just my online space to do with whatever I feel like. Here you can find links to my stuff, and I'll also post the (occasional) blog post on whatever I might be thinking about. Make yourself at home, and check out some of my cool friends by following the edges in the web ring!
