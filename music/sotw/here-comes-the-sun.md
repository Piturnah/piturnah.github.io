Song of the week! This is a project which my friends Anna and Sof have been doing since *forever*[^1]  and which I've repeatedly said I would join in on, but never actually started it --- until now! The catalyst for starting my own SotW has been finally getting around to ditching Spotify,[^2] as I'm now self-hosting a [navidrome](https://www.navidrome.org/) server for me and my partner.[^3] Setting up the infrastructure around this ---  such as [last.fm](https://www.last.fm/) for *scrobbling* stats --- has led to me spending a lot more time than usual thinking about music; and I decided I wanted to carve out a little space on this site to talk about it. So, here we are!

Our first SotW comes from [RAM](https://www.last.fm/music/Paul+&+Linda+McCartney/Ram), the debut album of Paul & Linda McCartney as musical collaborators. I've very long been a huge fan of the Beatles --- particularly of George --- but I had not looked particulary into Paul's solo work until my friend Amy recommended I check this album out. It's since become one of my favourite albums and has been playing almost on repeat.
